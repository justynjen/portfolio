Who am I?

About Me

A little over 2 years ago I left home and started traveling the world. At the same time, I started a travel blog. Over the years I learned that I loved editing and playing around with my website, as much as I loved writing on my blog and editing my photos. Then recently on a blogging retreat in Bali, I made friends with some pretty inspiring digital nomads, who showed me the power of web design. I chose to then attend a coding course with the Institute of Code. I was inspired by my mentors and the other students, to pursue a business of my own. I am now embarking on new adventures with the ability to fuse my love for writing and photography, with my love of web design. You can check out some websites that I have built below or ready my blog by going to www.adventuresofamiddlechild.com.

Some Websites

My Portfolio

This website I built for my aunt. She has a spray tanning business that she runs, to give her something to do as a stay at home mother. I reached out to her after my course to see if she would want a website where people could look at some before and after shots. It could also be a landing place as her small business continues to grow. I put on their some tips for someone getting their first spray tan, why you should choose an airbrushed tan and much more. It was so fun to work on this personal project with her and I am so excited to hear what her clients think of the site. Check it out by clicking below.

My Skills

My skills include building you a unique site that will have a fast load time and be mobile responsive. I build beautiful forms and quizes. Everything I do is geared towards pleasing your customers and driving new business to your site. I have a lot of knowledge about SEO and can help you reach new viewers through google. I can work with your deadline so that you will have a new site up an running in no time.
